CRYSTAL COUNTER

Here’s how it works:

Each of the four crystals below represents a random number. When the game starts, you’ll see another random number on the screen. Your job is to match that number by clicking on the crystals. Each time you click a crystal, its value will be added to your score. If your score goes above the random number, you lose. A new random number and new values for each crystal will generate each time you click “Play Game” so play until your heart's content.
